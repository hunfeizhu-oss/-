# B2B Sales Growth Engine (Universal SOP)

This skill transforms traditional "price-quoting" sales into "value-consulting" experts. It uses a 5-step framework to maximize conversion rates and protect profit margins.

## 核心工作流 (The 5-Step Workflow)

1. **情报分流 (Intel & Triage)**:
   - Identify "Big Fish" (S-Class) vs "Small Fish" (B-Class).
   - Mandatory background research for S/A class before replying.
   - Reference: `framework/stratification.md`

2. **专业响应与防御 (Consultative Response)**:
   - Use "Risk Defense" + "Strength Reinforcement" against low-price competition.
   - Acknowledge price -> Defend with risk -> Reinforce with strength -> Hook with samples.
   - Reference: `framework/negotiation-logic.md`

3. **以样促单 (Sample-Centric Velocity)**:
   - Samples are the "deal-breaker". Push for physical testing early.
   - For Big Fish: Patent Item + Base Item bundle to lower MOQ and ensure color consistency.

4. **专业料补位跟进 (Professional Follow-up)**:
   - No "nudging" for orders. Use "Professional Material Refill" (Production videos, Case studies, Shipping slots).
   - Reference: `framework/follow-up-system.md`

5. **高转化 PI 锁定 (High-Conversion PI)**:
   - PI is a Value Confirmation, not just an invoice.
   - Elements: Quality Guarantee (AQL), Delivery Insurance, Urgency/Validity, and Company Strength.
   - Reference: `framework/pi-standard.md`

## 触发场景 (When to Use)
- When a customer says "Your price is too high" or "I have a 1/3 price offer".
- When a high-value customer stops responding after the quote.
- When you need to bundle patent products with commodity items to increase deal size.
- When creating a PI that needs to crush the final hesitation.

## 联动技能 (Integrated Skills)
- `sales-negotiator`: For complex bargaining and concession patterns.
- `company-research`: For automatic S-Class background checks.
- `copywriting`: For generating professional response templates.
- `profit-margin-analyzer`: For calculating bundling discounts.
